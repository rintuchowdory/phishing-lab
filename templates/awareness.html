⚠️ Security Awareness Training

This was a phishing simulation.
You entered credentials into a fake login page.

What went wrong?

  • You trusted the page without verifying the URL
  • No Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) was used
  • The page looked legitimate

How to protect yourself

  • Always check the website address carefully
  • Use a password manager
  • Enable MFA on all accounts
  • Report suspicious emails immediately
